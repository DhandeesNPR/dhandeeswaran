Do's

✅ Buy the fireworks from the reliable sellers possessing licence.

✅ Always an adult should supervise the use of fireworks.

✅ Safety Precautions marked on the fireworks should be strictly followed.

✅ Use a candle or an agarbathi to ignite the fireworks.

✅ Always keep a buckets of water at hand to extinguish an incipient fire.

✅ Aerial fireworks should be used in a safe landing zone

DON'Ts

❎ Never ignite fireworks while holding them. Put them down then ignite them and walk away.

❎ Don't put fireworks in any container to ignite.

❎ Any malfunctioning fireworks should be abandoned.

❎ Never ignite aerial fireworks where overhead abstruction(tree, leaves, wires etc..) may interface with trajectory into open air space. Never ignite aerial fireworks near an opening to a building An open carrage door or window could allow aerial fireworks to fly into the structure and cause a fire.

❎ Never use fireworks inside house.

❎ Use fireworks outdoors, but not on the public throughfare.

❎ Never experiment or make your own fireworks.

❎ Never re-light a "dud" fireworks (wait 15 to 20 minutes and then soak it in a bucket of water).

❎ Do not use spurious / illegal fireworks.

❎ Fireworks should not be used between 10:00 pm & 6 am as per supreme court order.
